A complete garden transformation designed with curated plants, natural pathways, seasonal blooms, and peaceful green corners.

Location Surat, India
Service Garden Design
Timeline 4 Weeks

Project Overview

Creating a calm, green outdoor experience

The client wanted a fresh outdoor space that felt natural, peaceful, and easy to maintain throughout the year. Our approach focused on balancing open lawn areas with layered plantation, clean walking zones, and soft seasonal color.

We selected plants based on sunlight, watering needs, visual texture, and long-term maintenance. The final result is a garden that feels structured but still organic.

Work Completed

  • Landscape planning and layout
  • Plant selection and placement
  • Lawn development
  • Seasonal flower beds
  • Pathway and edge detailing
  • Maintenance guidance
